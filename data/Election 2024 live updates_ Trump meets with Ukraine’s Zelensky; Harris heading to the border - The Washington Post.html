Former president Donald Trump, who has been deeply skeptical of U.S. involvement in Ukraine, met with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky in New York on Friday. Speaking to reporters with Zelensky by his side, Trump suggested he would seek to broker an end to the war with Russia and said he has a “very good relationship” with Russian President Vladimir Putin. Later in the day, Trump is scheduled to campaign in Michigan. Vice President Kamala Harris, who met with Zelensky in Washington on Thursday, plans to head to Arizona, where she will visit the U.S.-Mexico border Friday. Polls have shown Trump to have an advantage on the issue of immigration.
